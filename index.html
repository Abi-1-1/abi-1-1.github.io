Popular Salary and Paycheck Questions

How much is $20 an hour per year?

Working 40 hours per week for 52 weeks, $20 an hour equals $41,600 per year. Use our Hourly to Salary Calculator to estimate any wage.

How do I convert annual salary to hourly pay?

Divide annual salary by the number of weeks worked per year, then by hours worked per week. Our Salary to Hourly Calculator does this automatically.

How can I estimate take-home pay?

Subtract taxes from gross income. Our Take-Home Pay Calculator gives a simple after-tax estimate based on the tax rate you enter.

Are these calculators exact?

These tools provide simplified estimates helpful for planning, comparison, and quick calculations. Actual taxes and deductions may vary by job and location.
